What's on the floor

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

A gym built for people who train on a schedule — before work, after work, or during the quiet hours in between.

01

Full free-weight floor

Squat racks, platforms, and plates loaded up to 25kg — no waiting on a single dumbbell rack.

02

Certified coaching

Every trainer on staff is certified and assigned to a member load they can actually track properly.

03

Biometric check-in

Fingerprint or QR entry logs your visit automatically — no queue at the front desk at 6am.

04

Free assessment

Weight, BMI, body fat, and a goals conversation before you commit to a single plan.

05

Lockers & showers

Personal locker included from the Standard plan up, so you can train on your way to work.

06

Early & late hours

Floor opens at 5:00am and stays open past most people's dinner. Train around your day, not ours.
